How to Play Dou Dizhu

Dou Dizhu is a 3-player card game where one player (the Landlord) plays against the other two (Farmers).

Game Phases

  1. Dealing: Each player gets 17 cards, 3 cards are placed face down.
  2. Bidding: Players bid to become the Landlord.
  3. Landlord Reveal: The winner gets the 3 hidden cards.
  4. Playing: Players take turns playing cards following the pattern.

Card Patterns

  • Single card
  • Pair (two cards of same rank)
  • Triplet (three cards of same rank)
  • Bomb (four cards of same rank)
  • Rocket (Big Joker + Small Joker)
